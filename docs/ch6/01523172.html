If your stocks moved in lockstep, portfolio volatility would equal the weighted average of individual volatilities (29.9%). Because they do not move together perfectly, your actual portfolio volatility is 14.5% - diversification eliminated 52% of the average stock's risk (Figure 6.1 in the chapter). What remains is mostly systematic risk.
