If your stocks moved in lockstep, portfolio volatility would equal the weighted average of individual volatilities (32.3%). Because they do not move together perfectly, your actual portfolio volatility is 16.6% - diversification eliminated 48% of the average stock's risk (Figure 6.1 in the chapter).
